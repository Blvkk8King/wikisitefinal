National Basketball Association
  • Sport: Basketball
  • Founded: June 6, 1946; 76 years ago (as BAA), New York City, New York, U.S
  • Inaugural Season: 1946-47
  • Commissioner: Adam Silver
  • No. of Teams: 30
  • Countries: U.S. 29 teams Canada 1 team
  • Headquarters: 645 Fifth Avenue New York, NY, 10022 U.S.
  • Most Recent Champions: Golden State Warriors (7 titles)
  • Most Titles: Los Angles Lakers and Boston Celtics (17 titles)
  • T.V. Partners: U.S. : ABC/ESPN TNT NBA TV Canada: TST/TSN2 Sportsnet/Sportsnet One NBA TV CANADA
  • Official Website: nba.com
